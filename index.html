About Me

I'm a professional barber offering personalized haircuts from my home garage. I specialize in modern fades, classic cuts, and detailed beard grooming. My goal is to give my clients the confidence they deserve with precision styling. I started this business as a side project, cutting hair from my garage while refining my craft. Over time, I realized my passion for barbering and the impact it has on people's confidence. Looking ahead, I hope to turn this into a full-scale business, opening my own shop and eventually expanding to bring high-quality, affordable haircuts to a wider community.
